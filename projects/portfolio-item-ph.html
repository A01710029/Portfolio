Outreach:Patrones Hermosos

Teaching grade school girls to code

An outreach program within Tecnológico de Monterrey that encourages middle school and high school girls to code.

In the first edition of the program in Campus Querétaro, I participated as a teacher and mentor to the high school group, teaching them to code in Python. Through programming workshops, mentorship sessions, and presentations given by female professors of the university, we introduced the students to the possibilities of a career in STEM. Eight of the thirteen students in the high school group declared or enrolled into a STEM career the following year.
