Project:Spilt Milk

Creative writing magazine

The creative writing magazine for my high school, MAST Academy, composed of the collaboration of the various artists of the school.

During my senior year, I served as one of two Editors-in-Chief of the magazine's 25th edition. In this role, I oversaw the copy-editing of the chosen pieces, the pairing of art and writing, the layout design, and the printing of the final product.
